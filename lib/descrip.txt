<b>Shiny App Description</b>
The plot shows car make efficiency using number of cars with MPG > 22 each year.
The plot dots are shaded for years when the global price of oil exceeded $50 per barrel.
Use pull-down lists to choose another car maker.
Type or click to alter the range of years, from 1984 through 2016.
There are no input controls designed into the UI yet (e.g. years prior to 1984).
The refresh button will clear up most errors.
